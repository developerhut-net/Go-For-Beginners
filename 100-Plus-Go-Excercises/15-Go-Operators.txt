
15. Go Operators

Goal: Practice arithmetic, comparison, and logical operators.

1. Add, subtract, multiply, and divide two numbers. Print results.

2. Use the % operator to find the remainder of two numbers.

3. Compare two numbers with ==, !=, >, <. Print the results.

4. Use logical operators (&&, ||, !) with boolean variables.

5. Show how assignment operators work (+=, -=, *=) with an integer variable.
