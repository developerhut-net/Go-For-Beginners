
5. Go Constants

Goal: Learn how to declare and use constants.

1. Declare a constant PI = 3.14 and print it.

2. Create a constant country = "UAE" and print: "I live in UAE".

3. Show difference between const and var by declaring both and printing them.

4. Declare multiple constants (Red, Green, Blue) and print them.

5. Create a constant DaysInWeek = 7 and print: "There are 7 days in a week".
